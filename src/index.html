Andy Mccune

Andy McCune is the founder of Earth, a millennial-focused travel media company; a co-founder of Unfold, a design toolkit for storytellers; the founder of Coast9, an influencer marketing platform; and the founder of several e-commerce companies, including Christmas AF.

Previously, Andy was the co-founder and chief operating officer of The Blu Market, a digital advertising platform focused on mobile user acquisition with clients including Amazon, Lyft, Expedia, and Machine Zone. He was also the co-founder of Beta Labs, a technology company based in Montevideo, Uruguay that built consumer apps used by over 4 million people including Top Nine, Game Switch, and Want.

Andy is passionate about experience-based businesses, travel, music, photography, and architecture. He currently splits his time between New York City and Tulum, Mexico.
